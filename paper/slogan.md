# Paper Slogans

## Primary (use in abstract / introduction)

> FormulationOS elevates Scientific Workflows to first-class citizens,
> enabling heterogeneous scientific foundation models to be executed,
> persisted, refined, replayed, and traced under a unified operating layer.

## Alternative (use as a tagline)

> If processes are the fundamental abstraction in Linux, then
> Scientific Workflows are the fundamental abstraction in FormulationOS.

## Anti-deterministic-models principle (use in conclusion / discussion)

> FormulationOS does not promise deterministic models.
> It promises **deterministic evidence**.

## Targeted-venue summary

> We introduce FormulationOS, a Scientific Operating System for
> computational pharmaceutics that turns natural language into
> executable, reproducible, and traceable scientific workflows.
